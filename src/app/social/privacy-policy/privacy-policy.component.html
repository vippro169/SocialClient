Privacy Policy

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 to provide protection for your personal data. We only collect and use your data with your permission. In order to create an account at our site, you must read and agree to this Privacy Policy.

  • Information that you provide when using our site:

  • Information that others provide about you:

Purposes for collecting
  • Provide social network service: We collect and use your information to provide a social network where you can share information with others.

    • When there is a legal request according to the law and in complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679.
